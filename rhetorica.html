Rhetorica: Interactive Language Test

This is a full simulation of the professional speaking and listening test. You may practice as often as you'd like during this beta period.

Part I: Social Conversation (13 minutes)

Instructions: Greet the interviewer. Engage in conversation about the weather, local food, and your city. Gradually shift to complex topics like education or civic responsibility.

Part II: Topical Q&A (18 minutes)

Select 3 topics from the list and respond to questions based on logistics and opinions.

  • Public safety at mass gatherings
  • Digital infrastructure and government services
  • Hybrid work models and productivity
  • Freedom of the press in crisis situations
  • Migration and citizenship pathways

Part III: Information Gathering & Reporting (13 minutes)

Instructions: Ask the 'tester' 3 questions in the target language. Then, report their answers in English.
